OVERVIEW

PURPOSE

We are introducing this new activity to every people who wants to explore the nature and spend time with family and friends. Our objective is educate about the rivers available to raft, equipment available and show to the public this beauty sport.

AUDIENCE

What are the target customers?

In this project the audience that we want is young and old people between 16-40 years old, who their interest is the nature and enjoy a nice time with their closest people, having an income $40.000.

What do they want?

The clearly information that can be attractive for the public, potential customers adore a short and clearly answer to their requeriments. They expect bundles and inclusive services that can meet their needs.

What are their needs that are note being met?

Sometimes the flexibility is a problem for the customers, when you pay a service or package with a horary requeriments can be a problem, in this case we are offering packages with an insurance customized for customers.
